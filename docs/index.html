Our Story

Peppes Mexican Grill has been part of the community for many years, serving guests with great food and a welcoming atmosphere. Three years ago, the restaurant was purchased by the new owners, David and Oswaldo, who brought fresh energy and a deeper Mexican influence to the menu.

While Peppes has always been known for its Tex-Mex style dishes, we added more authentic Mexican flavors and recipes to create a unique blend of Tex-Mex favorites with a true Mexican touch.

Our goal is simple: to share the flavors we love and create food that brings people together, making every visit to Peppes Mexican Grill a delicious and memorable experience.
